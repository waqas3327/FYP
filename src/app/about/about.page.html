User guide

How can I post about lost or found item or person ?

In the main home page, press Add button on bottom right of the screen and then select option of post found or lost.
1) First select item or person.
2) Then select photo and click upload button.
3) Insert marker on map and click post button.

How can I view posts ?

There are separate buttons for Lost posts and Found posts on top right.

How can I chat with person who has posted found or lost item/person ?

Open post and click on view profile button then click on chat button on top right corner.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who can use People Assist App?

We don't have an age limit on the use of People Assist App, however parents are encouraged to monitor what young children do online and on mobile devices.

Should I meet strangers to retrieve my lost stuff?

We encourage utilizing public spaces to meet strangers. Some cities have designated social media meeting spots around police departments. Use caution, as you would with any stranger encounter.

Does People Assist App charge a fee for rewards people ask or want to give to retrieve lost & found items?

People Assit app does not charge any fee as per now but maybe in near future it does.

I found dangerous and prohibited items. Am I allowed to post them so their owners can retrieve them?

NO, you are not allowed to post dangerous and harmful items if you found any. Serious action can be taken agaist user to do so.

I forgot my password, what do I do?

See option of forgot password from the app login screen.

Where are my posts? I can't find them on the map.

First make sure you have an active internet connection on your device. Go to you profile and look under My Posts
